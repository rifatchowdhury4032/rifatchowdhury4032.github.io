A little about me

I'm Md. Nafiul Kabir Chowdhury Rifat, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at World University of Bangladesh. Alongside my studies, I work as an Assistant Pharmacist at Square Hospital Limited, where I've learned how much precision, patience, and communication matter in a healthcare setting.

Balancing a business degree with hospital work has taught me how to manage time carefully and stay organized under pressure — skills that carry over into everything I do, including building this portfolio from scratch using HTML and CSS.
